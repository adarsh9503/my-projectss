Cloud Gaming Help & Support

Contact Support

If you encounter any issues or have questions, you can reach out to our support team through the following channels:

  • Email: support@cloudgamingservice.com (Response time: Within 24 hours)
  • Live Chat: Available 24/7 on our website. Click the chat icon at the bottom right corner to get started.
  • Phone Support: Call us at +1-800-GAME-HELP (Hours: Monday - Friday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM local time)
  • Community Forum: Join our gaming community to discuss and resolve issues with other users. Visit community.cloudgamingservice.com.

Troubleshooting Tips

Before reaching out, try these common troubleshooting steps:

  • Connection Issues: Ensure your internet speed meets the minimum requirement of 15 Mbps. Restart your router if needed.
  • Game Lag: Close unnecessary applications on your device and lower the streaming quality in the settings.
  • Login Problems: Reset your password or clear your browser cache.

For more detailed guides, visit our Help Center.

For further assistance, please contact us at support@cloudgamingservice.com.
